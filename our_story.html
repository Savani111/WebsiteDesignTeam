Our Band

Stage Fright was formed in the late 2010s when guitarist Alex Donovan and drummer Sam Carter bonded over their love for progressive and experimental rock at a local music venue. Inspired by bands like Pink Floyd and early Radiohead, they began crafting ambient, rhythmically complex instrumental tracks at a local library's media studio. Soon, bassist Clara Woods and keyboardist Lucas Hayes joined, enriching their sound and completing the lineup.

The band's name reflects their shared vulnerability, a theme they confront through music. To emphasize this, they wear masks during performances, encouraging audiences to focus solely on the music's emotional depth and innovative sound rather than the performers’ identities.

Known for blending cinematic soundscapes with introspective lyrics, Stage Fright quickly gained a following with their boundary-pushing approach to rock. Their music continues to evolve, offering a unique fusion of storytelling and sonic experimentation that challenges the limits of the genre.

Our Members

Clara Woods – Bassist

Clara Woods anchors Stage Fright with deep, resonant bass lines, providing melodic richness and harmonic stability. Her playing style is dynamic, seamlessly shifting between powerful, driving bass grooves and more atmospheric, subtle textures. Clara also adds an element of vocal harmony to the group's music, occasionally contributing background vocals, which adds a layer of emotional resonance to the band's more ethereal moments. Her versatility on the bass allows Stage Fright to explore various musical landscapes, from post-punk to atmospheric prog.

Lucas Hayes – Keyboardist & Synthesizer

Lucas Hayes is the sonic architect of Stage Fright's atmospheric sound, using an array of keyboards, synthesizers, and electronic effects to create lush, otherworldly textures. His keyboard playing is both a melody and an instrument of sound manipulation, creating a deep sense of space and ambiance in the band's music. He loves to blend classical piano with modern synthesizer technology, allowing the band to experiment with different genres, from ambient and psychedelic to symphonic and electronic.

Alex Donovan – Lead Guitarist & Songwriter

Alex Donovan is the creative force behind Stage Fright's captivating sound, known for his intricate guitar work and atmospheric compositions. His songwriting is central to the band's identity, as he explores themes of isolation, self-doubt, and existential questioning. Outside of Stage Fright, Alex is a passionate music theory student and enjoys experimenting with different genres, often incorporating jazz and classical music elements into his work.

Sam Carter – Drummer & Percussionist

Sam Carter is the rhythmic backbone of Stage Fright, providing the band with both precision and flair. His drumming style blends progressive rock's odd-time signatures with a deeply groovy, almost hypnotic feel. Sam is known for his ability to balance technical complexity with subtle emotional expression. He plays with a broad range of percussion instruments, often incorporating electronic drums and samples to add an experimental edge to Stage Fright's music.
