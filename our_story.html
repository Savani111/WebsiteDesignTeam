Our Band

Stage Fright was formed in the late 2010s when guitarist Alex Donovan and drummer Sam Carter bonded over their love for progressive and experimental rock at a local music venue. Inspired by bands like Pink Floyd and early Radiohead, they began crafting ambient, rhythmically complex instrumental tracks at a local library's media studio. Soon, bassist Clara Woods and keyboardist Lucas Hayes joined, enriching their sound and completing the lineup. The band's name reflects their shared vulnerability, a theme they confront through music. To emphasize this, they wear masks during performances, encouraging audiences to focus solely on the music's emotional depth and innovative sound rather than the performersâ€™ identities. Known for blending cinematic soundscapes with introspective lyrics, Stage Fright quickly gained a following with their boundary-pushing approach to rock. Their music continues to evolve, offering a unique fusion of storytelling and sonic experimentation that challenges the limits of the genre.
