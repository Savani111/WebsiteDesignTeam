Our Band

Stage Fright was formed in the late 2010s when guitarist Alex Donovan and drummer Sam Carter bonded over
their love for progressive and experimental rock at a local music venue. Inspired by bands like Pink
Floyd and early Radiohead, they began crafting ambient, rhythmically complex instrumental tracks at a
local library's media studio. Soon, bassist Clara Woods and keyboardist Lucas Hayes joined, enriching
their sound and completing the lineup.

The band's name reflects their shared vulnerability, a theme they confront through music. To emphasize
this, they wear masks during performances, encouraging audiences to focus solely on the music's
emotional depth and innovative sound rather than the performersâ€™ identities.

Known for blending cinematic soundscapes with introspective lyrics, Stage Fright quickly gained a
following with their boundary-pushing approach to rock. Their music continues to evolve, offering
a unique fusion of storytelling and sonic experimentation that challenges the limits of the genre.

Our Members

Clara Woods - Bassist

Clara Woods anchors Stage Fright with deep, resonant bass
lines, providing melodic richness and harmonic stability.
Her playing style is dynamic, seamlessly shifting between
powerful, driving bass grooves and more atmospheric, subtle
textures. Clara also adds an element of vocal harmony to
the group's music, occasionally contributing background
vocals, which adds a layer of emotional resonance to the
band's more ethereal moments. Her versatility on the bass
allows Stage Fright to explore various musical landscapes,
from post-punk to atmospheric prog.
